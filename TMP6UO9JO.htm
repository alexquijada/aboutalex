ABOUT ME PROJECTS













Background

I am an Arizona native and Arizona State University Graduate. Following my graduation from ASU, I decided to apply for graduate school at the University of Arizona and enrolled in their architecture program. After 2 1/2 years, I decided that though I find architecture intruiguing, it wasn't my true calling. That journey led me to Lexington, Kentucky and my first job as a Content Administrator/Graphic Designer at the University of Kentucky


User Experience

During my time at UK, I developed the site map and userflows, re-structured the information architecture, created graphic standards for the site, all in the hopes of making the site more user friendly for students


Empathy Comes Easy to Me

My 5 years in architecture have molded me to think of the user's needs first and design second. I strongly believe that a winning user experience is based off of solving the user's needs first
